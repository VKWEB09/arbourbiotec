PRODUCT PORTFOLIO

Arbour Biotec offers a comprehensive portfolio of Neuro-Psychiatry and specialty healthcare products developed to support healthcare professionals and improve patient outcomes across India.

OVERVIEW

Comprehensive Pharmaceutical Portfolio

Since its establishment, Arbour Biotec has focused on delivering innovative and reliable pharmaceutical formulations that address evolving healthcare challenges.

Our portfolio includes products across Neuro Psychiatry, Anti Depression, Anti Epileptic, Mood Stabilizers, Neuron Regenerators, Anti Migraine, Anti Parkinsonism, PPI's and De Addiction therapies.

In addition to Arbour Biotec’s Neuro-Psychiatry portfolio, our associated company Orienta Life Sciences Pvt. Ltd. strengthens our healthcare offerings through its specialized divisions Kardia and Diabliss, focusing on cardiovascular and diabetic care solutions. Together, both organizations provide a broader range of pharmaceutical products designed to address diverse therapeutic needs.

About Us

Delivering Trusted Pharmaceutical Solutions Since 2005

Company Overview:

Arbour Biotec Pvt. Ltd. is a trusted pharmaceutical company established in 2003 in Ahmedabad, Gujarat. Over the years, the organization has grown into a recognized name in the pharmaceutical industry with a strong commitment to quality, innovation, and patient care. The company is WHO-GMP certified and operates in compliance with regulatory standards to ensure the delivery of safe, effective, and reliable healthcare solutions.

The registered office of the company is located at B-213, Popular Centre, Someshwar Complex-1, Shyamal Cross Road, Satellite, Ahmedabad, Gujarat, while its corporate office is situated in Chandigarh. Since its inception, Arbour Biotec has focused on building a strong presence in the pharmaceutical sector and has achieved consistent growth through its dedication to excellence and customer satisfaction.

Arbour Biotec specializes in the Neuro-Psychiatry segment and offers an extensive portfolio of more than 170 pharmaceutical products. The company’s product range includes Anti-Psychotics, Anxiolytics, Anti-Depressants, Anti-Epileptics, Mood Stabilizers, Neuron Regenerators, Anti-Migraine therapies, Anti-Parkinsonism medicines, PPIs, and De-Addiction formulations. This diversified portfolio enables the company to address a wide range of therapeutic requirements while supporting healthcare professionals in delivering effective patient care.

To maintain the highest standards of manufacturing quality, Arbour Biotec collaborates with reputed manufacturing partners, including Akums Drugs & Pharmaceuticals Ltd., one of India’s leading pharmaceutical manufacturers. Through stringent quality control measures and regulatory compliance, the company ensures that every product meets established industry standards.

With a strong Pan-India presence, Arbour Biotec serves healthcare institutions, hospitals, and medical professionals across the country. The company is also an approved supplier to several reputed institutions, including Christian Medical College Ludhiana, Medanta Hospital Gurgaon, Metro Hospital Faridabad, the Department of Psychiatry at Government Medical College Jammu, and Artemis Hospital Gurgaon.

Driven by a vision to improve healthcare outcomes and enhance the quality of life for patients, Arbour Biotec continues to expand its reach through innovation, ethical business practices, and an unwavering commitment to pharmaceutical excellence. The company remains dedicated to delivering high-quality healthcare solutions that contribute to a healthier future for communities across India.

Our Associated Company – Orienta Life Sciences Pvt. Ltd.

Orienta Life Sciences Pvt. Ltd. is a subsidiary of Arbour Biotec Pvt. Ltd., established with a vision to provide high-quality pharmaceutical solutions backed by innovation, technology and patient-focused healthcare. Headquartered in Ahmedabad, Gujarat, the company operates with WHO-GMP compliant manufacturing standards and is committed to delivering safe, effective and reliable pharmaceutical products across India.

Orienta Life Sciences has established two specialized divisions, Kardia and Diabliss, dedicated to addressing the diverse healthcare requirements of patients and medical professionals. These focused divisions enable the company to deliver specialized pharmaceutical solutions across key therapeutic segments while ensuring comprehensive product availability and reliable healthcare support.

As a technology-driven pharmaceutical company, Orienta Life Sciences leverages advanced drug delivery systems and innovative formulation technologies. The company offers a wide range of specialized dosage forms including Tablet-in-Tablet, Tablet-in-Capsule, Mouth Dissolving Tablets (MDTs), Sublingual Tablets, Controlled Release (CR), Sustained Release (SR), Modified Release (MR), and Extended Release (ER) formulations.

These advanced technologies are designed to enhance product stability, bioavailability, quality, patient compliance, and therapeutic effectiveness, ensuring reliable healthcare outcomes and consistent product performance.

The company has built its reputation through a strong focus on quality, consistency, and advanced pharmaceutical manufacturing practices. Through its commitment to innovation and excellence, Orienta Life Sciences continues to expand its presence in the pharmaceutical sector while supporting healthcare professionals with trusted and effective healthcare solutions.

Orienta Life Sciences also actively supports medical education initiatives, academic programs, CME activities, healthcare conferences, and awareness programs that contribute to the advancement of the healthcare and pharmaceutical industries across India.

As a growing pharmaceutical organization, Orienta Life Sciences remains committed to improving patient well-being through quality-driven operations, ethical business practices, innovative healthcare solutions, and long-term relationships with healthcare providers nationwide.
